How Our Matcher Works

Our pet matcher considers multiple factors to recommend the best pet for your lifestyle:

  • Pet Size: Matches your space and preferences
  • Activity Level: Aligns with your energy and exercise habits
  • Indoor/Outdoor: Considers where your pet will spend most time
  • Housing: Factors in your living situation
  • Experience: Accounts for your pet care expertise
  • Time Available: Matches pets to your daily schedule
